Design Language

  • ◆ Nothing Phone meets Apple MagSafe. Transparent/frosted aesthetic with clean industrial lines. No visible screws, no chunky plastic — just a slim panel that looks like it belongs on the phone.
  • ◆ Flush rails, not clips. The spring mechanism is hidden inside matte aluminum side rails that mirror the phone's own edge profile. When attached, it looks like a sleek phone case extension — not a clip-on accessory.
  • ◆ Open call window at the bottom. A ~60px open zone below the shield lets you see incoming calls and swipe to answer without removing the device. You stay reachable. The shield blocks the feed, not your phone.
  • ◆ 2mm total thickness. Adds almost nothing to the phone's profile. Slips into a pocket. Doesn't catch on anything.
  • ◆ Matte everything. No fingerprint magnets. Soft-touch coating on rails. Frosted (not glossy) shield. Feels premium in hand.
