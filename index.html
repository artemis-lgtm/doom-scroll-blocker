The Competitive Streak

How It Works

  • 1 Clip it on. Squeeze the spring rails, slide onto your phone over any case. The frosted shield covers your feed. The LCD display reads 000y 000d.
  • 2 The timer starts. A built-in segment LCD counts up in years and days. No app. No charging. No Bluetooth. Just a physical counter that ticks relentlessly.
  • 3 Compete with friends. "I'm on day 47. You?" The streak is visible, physical, and impossible to fake. It's right there on the front of your phone.
  • 4 Cheat = reset. A light sensor behind the shield detects removal. The instant you take the blocker off, light floods the sensor and the timer snaps back to 000y 000d. Your streak is dead. Start over.
  • 5 You keep your phone. Notifications stay visible at the top. Calls come through at the bottom. You just can't scroll. That's the point.
Anti-Cheat: Light Sensor Reset

Tamper Detection

  • * Phototransistor, $0.02-0.05. Sits between shield and screen. Paper-thin, zero moving parts, draws zero current in the dark.
  • * Can't cheat in the dark? You could theoretically remove it in a pitch-black room. But you can't use your phone with the screen off. The screen's own backlight triggers the sensor.
  • * Instant and permanent. No grace period. One photon and your 47-year streak is gone. The psychological weight of that is the product.

Design Philosophy

  • * Physical > Digital. Every competitor uses apps and software. We use a piece of frosted plastic with a light sensor. You can't hack physics. You can't uninstall a clip.
  • * The streak IS the product. The LCD display isn't a feature -- it's the entire value proposition. The social pressure of a visible counter that resets on cheating is what keeps it on.
  • * No app, no account, no subscription. Clip it on. It works. Forever. The CR2025 battery outlasts most friendships.
  • * Flush rails, not chunky clips. Spring mechanism hidden inside slim aluminum rails. Looks like a premium phone case extension.
  • * 2mm shield + 1mm electronics = 3mm total. Barely changes the phone's profile. Pocketable.

Go-To-Market Angle

  • * TikTok-first. "My doom scroll blocker's been on for 47 days. My friend cracked at 12." The streak comparison IS the content. Every reset is a viral moment.
  • * Friend packs. Sell 2-packs and 4-packs at a discount. The product only works competitively. Buying one alone is fine but buying two is a challenge.
  • * No subscription moat needed. Physical product = one-time purchase = simple economics. Reorders come from gifting and replacements.
  • * The reset sound. If we add a tiny piezo buzzer ($0.03), the blocker can emit a shame beep when the timer resets. That sound becomes the brand.
