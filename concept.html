Your streak is visible. Physical. Impossible to fake.

Every screen time app can be deleted. Every digital blocker can be bypassed. Every lock box takes your phone away entirely.

Scroll Shield is different. It's a frosted polycarbonate panel that clips over your phone screen. You keep your phone. Notifications stay visible at the top. Calls come through at the bottom. You just can't scroll.

The built-in LCD streak timer counts every day the shield stays on. Remove it -- even for a second -- and the light sensor resets your counter to zero. Your 47-day streak? Gone. Start over.

That's the product. The fear of losing your streak is stronger than the urge to scroll.

How It Works
01
Clip it on. Spring-loaded aluminum rails grip any phone from 60-90mm wide -- naked or in a case. One SKU fits everything from iPhone SE to Galaxy Ultra.
02
The timer starts. A custom segment LCD begins counting up in years and days. No app. No Bluetooth. No charging. Just a CR2025 coin cell that lasts 3-5 years.
03
Keep your phone. The top stays open for notifications and the clock. The bottom has a call window so you can see and answer incoming calls. You lose the feed, not the phone.
04
Compete with friends. "I'm on day 47. You?" The streak is right there on the front of your phone. Visible, physical, and impossible to fake. Buy a 2-pack and challenge someone.
05
Cheat = reset. A phototransistor behind the shield detects light. Remove the blocker and your streak snaps back to 000y 000d. The screen's own backlight triggers it -- you can't even cheat in the dark.
